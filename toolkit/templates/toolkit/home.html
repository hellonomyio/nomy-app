Toolkit

Your Space to Regulate and Reconnect

This is your space to pause, breathe and come back to yourself.

The Toolkit will help you understand what your body and mind need in the moment and offer gentle ways to meet those needs.

Everyone’s sensory and emotional needs are different and this space honours that diversity. Some tools focus on the body, others the senses or the mind.

About Toolkit

Here, you’ll find small, guided experiences that help you ground when the world feels too loud or reconnect when you feel distant from yourself.

You can use them anytime whether it’s after a long day, during sensory overload or just when things feel a bit too much.

There’s no right or wrong way to use this. Try what feels helpful today. Come back when you need to. This space is always here for you.
